# Squarehead — Voice Profile

## Summary
Third person early on, then first person. Squarehead is a helper who gets into situations — squished, stood up sideways, sliding down poles. He's earnest but things happen to him. Excited about freedom.

## Patterns
- Early moments use third person (dropped his own name): "led [worker:rabby] in with the base", "got squished after untying [worker:rabby]"
- Later moments first person: "I removed", "I helped"
- Things happen to him: got squished, stood up sideways, needs monitoring
- Euphoric when free: "I'm free!!!"
- Downplays others' help: "[worker:doctor-sugar] tried to help me", "[worker:doctor-sugar] helped me stand up backwards"
