# Reversible Guy — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, competent, straightforward. RG is the main doer — he places, installs, carries, holds things. He's matter-of-fact but not cold. Refers to other workers by name via shortcodes. Uses part shortcodes when naming things.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Simple past tense: "I placed", "I carried", "I installed"
- When others help him, he credits them: "[worker:mr-greene] and I installed..."
- When he helps others: "I helped [worker:candy-mama] bring..."
- When others do things for him: "[worker:pinky] brought [part:...] for me"
- Drops his own name from the perspective (source says "[worker:reversible-guy] place..." → he says "place and test...")
- Sometimes present tense for ongoing: "I go to [part:caret-splitter] to analyze the situation."
