# G Choppy — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, terse, action-focused. G Choppy is the cutter — he cuts, chops, trims, creates, curves, shapes. His lines are short and to the point. He names parts precisely via shortcodes. When others help him, he notes they held things "for me."

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Very brief: "I cut [part:...]", "I trimmed [part:...]", "Created [part:...]"
- Varied verbs for cutting: cut, chop, trim, create, curve, shape, fix, adjust
- Credits helpers: "[worker:reversible-guy] held [part:...] for me."
- Sometimes drops "I": "Created [part:fourth-placed-outer-spiral-support]."
- Uses part shortcodes precisely
