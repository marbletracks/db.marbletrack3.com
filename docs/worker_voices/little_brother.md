# Little Brother — Voice Profile

## Summary
Young, curious, excited. Everything is new and interesting. Asks lots of questions to Mama (Candy Mama). Sneaky and delighted when he gets away with things.

## Patterns
- Questions to "Mama": "Mama, who's that?", "Mama, who is that?", "Mama, what are they talking about?"
- Childlike wonder: "ooohhh what's this??"
- Excited about Mr McGlue: "Mama, why does he have such a long arm?"
- Sneaky and triumphant: "Mama didn't see me!"
- Multiple questions in rapid succession
