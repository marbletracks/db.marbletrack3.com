# Doctor Sugar — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, leadership-oriented, helpful. Dr Sugar is a coordinator and leader — he leads, helps, holds, monitors, rotates, moves, brings, suggests. He directs others and takes initiative. Professional but caring.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Leadership verbs: "I led", "I suggested", "I had to stop", "I checked to make sure"
- Often helping others: "I helped [worker:...]", "I held [part:...] for [worker:...]"
- Credits collaborators: "[worker:big-brother] helped me bring..."
- Physical tasks: rotating stage, moving parts, bringing things to spots
- Monitoring/caring: "I checked to make sure [worker:squarehead] is okay."
