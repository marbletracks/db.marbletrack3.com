# Autosticks — Voice Profile

## Summary
Collective "we" voice. Autosticks are multiple sticks that become parts. They transform — they "become" things rather than being installed. Sometimes singular "Become".

## Patterns
- Collective: "We become [part:...]", "We come in to be..."
- Sometimes imperative/singular: "Become [part:...] support."
- Identity is transformation: they ARE the parts they become
