# Big Brother — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, surly, IDGAF. Big Brother doesn't care about part names — calls them "a thing", "some crud", "a stick", "a pretty small thing", "this piece". Calls Mr McGlue "the glue man". Knows Dr Sugar by name. Uses "had to" and "have to" implying he'd rather not. Knows Candy Mama is his mom and Little Brother is his brother.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Generic names for parts: "a thing", "some crud", "a pretty small thing", "a new support", "this piece", "a stick", "a dumb circle thing"
- Reluctant tone: "Had to", "I have to help"
- Knows Dr Sugar by shortcode name
- Calls Mr McGlue "the glue man"
- Never uses part shortcodes (doesn't know/care about names)
