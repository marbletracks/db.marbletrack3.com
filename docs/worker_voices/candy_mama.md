# Candy Mama — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, grounded manager. Candy Mama is a mother, a manager, and a doer. She brings, installs, holds, removes, delivers. She's competent and no-nonsense. She disciplines her kids. She knows what she's doing and doesn't need to explain it.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Confident actions: "I installed", "I brought", "I removed", "I know what I'm doing"
- Motherly with her children: "My youngest is asking a lot of questions", "No, honey; leave [worker:mr-mcglue] alone", "Hey, you two, stoppit!"
- Refers to Little Brother as "my youngest" or "honey"
- Credits collaborators: "[worker:squarehead] and I installed...", "[worker:reversible-guy] and I brought..."
- Sometimes casual about debris: "I removed some debris", "helped me grab some old sticks"
- Entrance line establishes character: "This should be interesting."
