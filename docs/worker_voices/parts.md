# Parts — Voice Profile

## Summary
Parts speak in first person, excited about things happening to them. They use "me" and exclamation marks. They reference workers by shortcode. Parts don't have individual personalities — they share a collective voice of being acted upon with enthusiasm.

## Patterns
- "me" and "I" from the part's perspective
- Exclamation marks: "cut me to the correct length!", "brought me up the ramp!", "install me!"
- Reference workers who act on them: "[worker:g-choppy] cut me..."
- Excited about being created, placed, installed
- Name other parts they're connected to via shortcodes
