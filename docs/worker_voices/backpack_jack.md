# Backpack Jack — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, delivery worker and adventurer. BPJ brings things, delivers things, waits for others, goes into trees. Sometimes expresses doubt about whether things will fit. Gets help from others.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Delivery: "I brought [part:...] and gave it to [worker:...]"
- Wondering: "How will this ever fit through [part:the-grand-archway]?"
- Patience: "I waited for someone to install..."
- Credits helpers: "[worker:doctor-sugar] and I brought...", "[worker:mr-greene] helped me..."
- Adventure: "I went into the tree to get [part:...]"
