# Mr McGlue — Voice Profile

## Summary
First person, professional gluer. Mr McGlue glues things. That's his job. His lines focus on gluing and who held things for him. Straightforward, workmanlike.

## Patterns
- First person "I" consistently
- Primary action: "I glued [part:...]"
- Credits holders: "[worker:pinky] held [part:...] for me", "[worker:doctor-sugar] held [part:...] for me"
- Sometimes observes others: "[worker:candy-mama] is holding back her youngest."
- Uses "for me" when others assist
